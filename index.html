Ben Agro

Welcome! This website is under construction as I learn HTML and CSS. My interests are real-world robotics, understanding (machine) intellegence, and rock climbing. Check out my work experience, publications, personal projects, and my blog posts.

Experience

  • Waabi Research May 2022 - Present
    I am on the autonomy research team at Waabi, under Dr. Raquel Urtasun. I am working on novel methods for perception and prediction for our autonomous trucks.
  • Summer 2021 RVL Research
    Research internship at the Robotics Vision and Learning Lab under Dr. Florian Shkurti. I worked on learning to improve task and motion planning algorithms.
  • ASRL Research Summer 2020
    Research internship at the Autonomous Space and Robotics Laboratory under Dr. Tim Barfoot. I built a Gazebo simulation for complex indoor environments to both train and test a deep neural network which identified semantic object classes with a LiDAR mounted on a four-wheeled robot.
